C.L. Haskell & Son electricians is a family-owned business which has been serving the Blue Hill Peninsula and the islands of Penobscot Bay for more than thirty years. From small repairs to large construction projects, our experienced electricians are here to provide you with dependable, courteous service.

Founded in 1983 by Curtis Haskell, our business has grown to include three generations as well as a staff of experienced and knowledgeable master electricians. We provide electrical service to Stonington, Deer Isle, Blue Hill, Sedgwick, Brooksville, Brooklin, and the islands of Penobscot Bay.
